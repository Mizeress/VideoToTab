# Contribution Note
Great! You want to contribute to making tablature available to the masses! Before you contribute, please read what my philosophy for this project is on sustainability and licensing.

## Philosophy
I believe for a project reach the most people, it needs to be two things:
1. **Free & Open**: This product should be free for people to use and change as they please, and it should remain available to everyone under the AGPLv3.
2. **Integrated**: Available for use in professional music suites that can't technically comply with AGPLv3.

To achieve this, I use a **Dual-License model** to help keep the product available to everyone. 

## Contribution Agreement
By submitting code to this project, you agree to the following:
1. Open Source: Your contribution will be licensed to the public under the GNU AGPLv3.
2. Commercial Rights: You grant the project owner the right to include your contribution in proprietary versions sold to third parties.

If you have concerns about this model or want to discuss a specific high-value contribution, please reach out! I value your labor and want this to feel fair to everyone involved.
